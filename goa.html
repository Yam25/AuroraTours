7 Days Trip

Experience the Vibrant Spirit of Goa

Itinerary

We understand that every traveler is unique! This itinerary can be customized to suit your needs. Contact us for personalized options!

Day 1: Arrival in Goa

  • Arrive at Goa International Airport and transfer to your hotel.
  • Relax on the beach and enjoy a sunset view.
  • Dinner at a beach shack serving local Goan cuisine.

Day 2: North Goa Beaches & Nightlife

  • Visit popular beaches like Anjuna, Vagator, and Baga.
  • Enjoy water sports such as parasailing and jet skiing.
  • Explore the local markets.
  • Dinner at a beach club.
  • Experience the vibrant nightlife at clubs like Tito's or Café Mambo.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration

  • Visit the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral.
  • Explore the vibrant streets of Fontainhas in Panjim.
  • Enjoy dinner at a local Goan restaurant.

Day 4: Wildlife and Nature

  • Visit the Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • Explore the scenic Dudhsagar Waterfalls.
  • Return to the hotel for a relaxing evening.

Day 5: Adventure Activities

  • Go for a spice plantation tour and lunch.
  • Experience river rafting on the Mhadei River.
  • Relax at your hotel or explore local nightlife.

Day 6: South Goa Beaches & Nightlife

  • Spend the day at Palolem Beach and relax in a hammock.
  • Enjoy dolphin spotting boat rides.
  • Dinner at a beach shack with live music.
  • Experience nightlife at clubs like Leopard Valley or Silent Noise.

Day 7: Departure

  • Last-minute shopping in local markets.
  • Transfer to Goa International Airport for departure.
