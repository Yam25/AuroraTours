Experience the Majesty of Manali

7-Day Manali Adventure

We understand that every traveler is unique! This itinerary can be customized to suit your needs. Contact us for personalized options!

Day 1: Arrival in Manali

  • Arrive in Manali; transfer to your hotel.
  • Rest and acclimatize to the weather.
  • Evening stroll in the local market.

Day 2: Solang Valley Adventure

  • Visit Solang Valley for paragliding and zorbing.
  • Enjoy a picnic lunch amidst beautiful scenery.
  • Return to Manali for a relaxing evening.

Day 3: Rohtang Pass Excursion

  • Take a day trip to Rohtang Pass.
  • Enjoy breathtaking views and snow activities.
  • Visit the local Dhaba for lunch.

Day 4: Kullu and Manikaran

  • Day trip to Kullu and Manikaran.
  • Visit the Kullu Shawl factories and local temples.
  • Experience the hot springs in Manikaran.

Day 5: Hadimba Temple and Vashisht Village

  • Visit the Hadimba Temple in the morning.
  • Explore Vashisht Village and its hot springs.
  • Enjoy a traditional Himachali meal at a local restaurant.

Day 6: Naggar Castle and Art Gallery

  • Visit Naggar Castle and enjoy panoramic views of the valley.
  • Explore the Roerich Art Gallery.
  • Return to Manali for leisure time.

Day 7: Departure

  • Last-minute shopping in Manali.
  • Transfer to the bus station for your journey home.
